About

Standing in the long queue waiting for your turn or just whatsapp your notes was not enough . The idea of this website would'nt came until we didn't had it's utmost need during the time of examinations. This is not just only to save your valuable time but it also keeps you updated with the notes being added to it with the excellent facility of providing Previous Papers. Now you don't have to wait for your turn in a long wide queue rather just order your notes online. It will also check the availability of your notes at different Xerox Stores provided with it's best and accurate Price. It also have a commendable feature of downloding your notes whenever you wish. It's not just ended yet ,this amazing website will also tableau you with it's BLOG PAGE and the new Upcoming Events.

Our Process

Firstly Sign Up. Search for your notes code or check if it's already there. After getting information about your notes, you have to check it's availability at different Xerox Stores. Order it online by just typing the Number of Copies. Pay online. Tell your generated secret code to the Shopkeeper to have your notes. If you want a soft copy then press the Download button.
